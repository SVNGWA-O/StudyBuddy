How connecting works

Simple, secure, and takes less than 2 minutes

1

Choose Your Platform

Select your school's LMS (Canvas, Blackboard, Moodle, etc.) or other tools you want to connect. We support all major platforms.

2

Authenticate Securely

You'll be redirected to your school's official login page. We never see your password — authentication happens directly with your institution.

3

Grant Permissions

Approve what Study Buddy can access (courses, assignments, grades). You have full control and can revoke access anytime.

4

Start Learning Smarter

That's it! Your courses, assignments, and materials are now synced. Our AI tutor knows your syllabus and is ready to help.

Industry-Standard OAuth 2.0: We use the same authentication protocol as Google, Microsoft, and major tech companies. Your credentials stay with your school — we only receive a secure access token.

Your privacy is our priority

We take data security seriously. Here's exactly how we protect your information.

Bank-Level Encryption

All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). The same encryption used by major financial institutions.

OAuth 2.0 Authentication

We never see your passwords. Authentication happens directly with your school using industry-standard OAuth protocols.

Read-Only Access

We only read your course information — we can't post, submit, or modify anything in your LMS. You stay in complete control.

FERPA Compliant

Fully compliant with student privacy laws (FERPA, COPPA, GDPR). Your educational records are protected by law.

You Control Your Data

Disconnect anytime with one click. Export or delete your data whenever you want. No questions asked.

No Selling Your Data

We will never sell your personal information to third parties. Ever. Your data helps you learn — not advertisers profit.
