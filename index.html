About Study Buddy

Rising to meet the changing needs of modern education

A New Era in Education

We are living in a transformative moment in education. The traditional model ‚Äî where learning is expensive, fragmented, and inaccessible to many ‚Äî is no longer sustainable. Students today face rising costs, disconnected tools, and a system that often works against them rather than for them.

Study Buddy was born from a simple belief: education should empower, not burden. We exist to support students at the right age, at the right moment, with the right tools ‚Äî making academic success achievable for everyone, regardless of their financial situation.

In the rise of this growing change in education, we stand with students. We are not just building technology; we are building a movement toward fairness, accessibility, and genuine support.

Our Mission

To make quality education accessible and affordable for every student by providing a free, AI-powered academic companion that removes financial barriers to learning, eliminates wasted time, and delivers personalized support that adapts to each student's unique needs and learning style.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every student ‚Äî regardless of background, income, or circumstance ‚Äî has access to intelligent, compassionate learning support. A world where academic success is determined by effort and understanding, not by the ability to afford expensive tutors or subscription services.

Our Goals

  • Support 1 million students in their academic journey by 2028
  • Reduce textbook costs by an average of 50% for all users
  • Save students a collective 10 million hours of study time annually
  • Partner with 500+ educational institutions to expand access
  • Maintain 100% free access to core AI tutoring features
